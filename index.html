The Problem We Solve

At RPI, musicians are everywhere—performers, composers, and producers—but finding the right people to play with is harder than it should be. Announcements get buried in group chats, replies are inconsistent, and it’s tough to match skill level, genre, and availability.

Resonance is a musician-run community that brings everything into one place: discover bandmates and ensembles, post or join practice sessions, coordinate rehearsals, and collaborate on projects and performances—so you spend less time searching and more time making music.
