Welcome to RPISocial

We tend to be glued to a cluster of Discord servers or group chats where we miss out on real human connection or relationships that would make campus life realer than ever. RPI is a constant vortex of hard courses, state-of-the-art research and large scale projects. However, in a community of thousands of intelligent individuals, even finding people who share the same interests with you, be it organizing a pop-up art sale, finding a partner to cram in some last-minute PHYS 1200 before an exam, or even finding people to participate in a basic discussion or debate, can be harder than it seems.
